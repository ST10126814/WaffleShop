House of Grazie specializes in fully customizable authentic waffles made with our signature pressed waffle technique. We offer both sweet and savory waffle creations that you won't find anywhere else. Our unique "build your own" approach lets you create the perfect waffle experience every time.

Yes! We offer gluten-free waffle batter and a variety of vegan toppings. Our team is trained to handle dietary restrictions and can help you create a delicious waffle that meets your needs. Please inform our staff about any allergies or dietary requirements when ordering.

We offer online ordering for both pickup and delivery through our website and various delivery platforms. You can customize your waffle order online just like you would in-store. Delivery availability depends on your location and our partner delivery services.

Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes! From corporate meetings to birthday parties, we can provide fresh waffles, wafflesticks, and all the fixings. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your catering needs and we'll create a custom package for your event.

Our hours vary, but most House of Grazie locations are open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week. Some locations may have extended hours or different weekend schedules. Check our locations page or call your local store for specific hours.

House of Grazie was founded with a mission to bring a unique twist to the classic waffle. Inspired by traditional Belgian recipes and a desire for innovation, we developed our signature "pressed waffle" technique. This method creates a perfectly crispy exterior while maintaining a light and fluffy interior, making it the ideal base for both sweet and savory creations.
