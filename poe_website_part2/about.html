About House Of Grazie

Our Story

House Of Grazie was founded in 2020 by passionate food lovers who wanted to bring a unique twist to the classic waffle. Inspired by traditional Belgian recipes and a desire for innovation, we developed our signature "pressed waffle" technique. This method creates a perfectly crispy exterior while maintaining a light and fluffy interior, making it the ideal base for both sweet and savory creations.

Our mission is simple: to serve delicious, high-quality waffles made with love and the finest ingredients, providing a memorable experience for every guest.
